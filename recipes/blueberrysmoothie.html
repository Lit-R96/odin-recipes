BLUEBERRY SMOOTHIE

Home

This vibrant blueberry smoothie is the perfect blend of refreshing, fruity flavor and creamy texture, ideal for a warm afternoon in a tropical setting. With its deep, inviting purple hue and a rich top layer of fluffy whipped cream, it's as beautiful as it is delicious. Finished with whole, fresh blueberries, a sprig of mint, and a cheerful striped straw, this drink is a simple, indulgent escape that’s both nourishing and deeply satisfying.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup fresh blueberries, chilled
  • 1 cup blueberry yogurt (e.g., Greek yogurt for extra protein)
  • 1 cup milk of your choice (whole, skim, or a plant-based alternative like almond or coconut milk)
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup or honey (optional, for added sweetness)
  • 1 cup ice cubes (optional, for a colder, thicker texture)
  • Whipped cream, for topping
  • Extra fresh blueberries and mint leaves, for garnish.

STEPS

  1. Preparation: Wash the fresh blueberries thoroughly and pat them dry. Chill them in the refrigerator or freezer for at least 15 minutes before starting. Measure out your yogurt and milk.
  2. Blend the Base: Place the chilled blueberries into your blender. Add the blueberry yogurt and the milk. If you prefer a colder and thicker smoothie, add the optional ice cubes at this stage.
  3. Sweeten and Mix: For a slightly sweeter taste, add the maple syrup or honey. Place the lid securely on the blender and blend the ingredients until they are perfectly smooth and creamy, with a rich purple color.
  4. Serve and Top: Pour the smoothie into a tall, chilled glass. Spoon or spray a generous amount of whipped cream on top, and use a straw to help create that classic layered effect.
  5. Garnish: Carefully place a handful of whole, fresh blueberries on top of the whipped cream, along with a sprig of mint for a touch of fresh green. Enjoy your perfect blueberry smoothie immediately!
